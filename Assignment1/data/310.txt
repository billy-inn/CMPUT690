Lucas Vázquez Iglesias (born 1 July 1991 in Curtis, Galicia, Province of A Coruña), simply Lucas, is a Spanish footballer who plays for Real Madrid Castilla as a right midfielder.
Lucas joined Real Madrid's youth system in 2007, aged 16. He made his professional debut with the B team on 21 August 2011, in a Segunda División B game against Sporting de Gijón B (1–0 home win).
