Yoel Rodríguez Oterino (born 28 August 1988 in Vigo, Pontevedra, Galicia), simply Yoel, is a Spanish footballer who plays for Celta de Vigo as a goalkeeper.
A product of local Celta de Vigo's youth ranks, Yoel was promoted to the first team for 2009–10, with the Galicians in the second division. On 10 February 2010, following a string of good performances in that year's Spanish Cup, he was called up to the Spain national under-21 football team training camp by manager Juan Ramón López Caro.
