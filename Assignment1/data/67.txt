Davide Zappacosta (born 23 June 1992) is an Italian footballer who plays for Atalanta.
Zappacosta started his professional career at Isola Liri, which he also played in Berretti under-20 team of Isola Liri. Before joining the Isola del Liri team, he played for another Lazio team Sora in Giovanissimi under-15 level. In January 2011 he joined Atalanta in co-ownership deal and played in its Primavera under-20 team. At the start of season he played in pre-season friendlies for Atalanta first team but was deemed to be surplus to its Serie A plan. On 31 August 2011 he left for Avellino.
