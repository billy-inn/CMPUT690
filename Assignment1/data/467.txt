Simon Makienok Christoffersen (born 21 November 1990) is a Danish professional association football whos plays a Centre Foward for Danish Superliga side Brøndby IF. He has played six games for the Denmark national under-21 football team.
Makienok joined Brøndby on 28 January 2012 for fee of £ 470,000, signing a 4 year contract and was given the number 9 shirt. He made his debut on 4 March 2012, in a 1–0 win against SønderjyskE. Makienok scored his first goal for Brøndby IF in a 2–1 win, against FC Nordsjælland. On 3 May 2012, in a 3–2 win against Silkeborg IF, Makienok scored 2 goals, before Mike Jensen made it 3–2, and Brøndby won.
