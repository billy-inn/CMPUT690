Marco D'Alessandro (born 17 February 1991 in Rome) is an Italian footballer who plays as a winger for Serie B club Verona, on loan from Roma.
D'Alessandro moved his first footsteps into Lazio youth system. In 2005 he joined Roma youth teams. On March 21, 2009 he made his senior debut in Serie A as an injury time substitute against Juventus, which Roma lost 4â€“1.
In July 2009 he was sent to Serie B side Grosseto on a season-long loan to gain first-team experience.
In July 2010 he was sent to Serie A side Bari. Then in January 2011 he was sent to Serie B side Livorno.
In August 2011 he was sent to Serie B club Verona.
On January 10, 2012 he scored a goal against Lazio in the Coppa Italia.
With the Italy U-19 he took part at the 2010 Euro U-19 Championship. On 17 November 2010 he made his debut with the Italy U-21 team in a friendly match against Turkey.
