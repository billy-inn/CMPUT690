Jordi Masip López (born 3 January 1989 in Sabadell, Barcelona, Catalonia) is a Spanish footballer who plays for FC Barcelona B as a goalkeeper.
Masip moved to FC Barcelona's La Masia in 2004, at the age of 15. He made his senior debuts in the 2008–09 season, playing with UE Vilajuïga - also in Catalonia - in the fourth division, on loan.
Subsequently, Masip returned to FC Barcelona B, serving as backup or third-choice during his first seasons.
