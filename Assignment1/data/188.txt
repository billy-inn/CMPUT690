Adrian Marius Stoian (born 11 February 1991 in Craiova) is a Romanian footballer who currently plays for Serie B side A.S. Bari on loan from A.S. Roma.
He made his Serie A debut on 21 March 2009 against Juventus F.C. as a substitute for Mirko Vučinić in the last minutes of the game.
On 5 August 2011 he joined A.S. Bari on a long season loan deal that bring teammates Alessandro Crescenzi and Simone Sini to the team too.
