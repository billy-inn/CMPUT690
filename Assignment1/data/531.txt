Antonio Rozzi (born May 28, 1994 in Rome, Italy) is an Italian footballer who plays as a striker for Serie A club Lazio.
Born in Rome, Rozzi began his career with local side Casal Monastero, before moving to A.S. Lodigiani. At Lodigiani, he was identified by Lazio, where he became a successful youth player.
Rozzi was first promoted to the Lazio first team during the 2011-12 season, making his Serie A debut in Lazio's first league win over Milan for 14 years. He came on as an injury time substitute for captain Tommaso Rocchi.
