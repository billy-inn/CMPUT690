ORGANIZATION1060 d'Italia is a stadium in LOCATION1059 , LOCATION42 , used by the ORGANIZATION48 and PERSON2097 football teams . The field is 120 m long , and 70 m wide . The stadium has officially 24,642 recognized seats . The stadium is seen by many to be outdated and not up to standard for Serie A due to poor facilities , poor views from a number of stands and because most of the stadium has no roof cover . In the last decade some projects for a new stadium have been proposed but as of February 2010 no project has been carried out .
