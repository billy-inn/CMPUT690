José Rodolfo Pires Ribeiro, better known as Dodô, is a Brazilian defender who currently plays for Corinthians.
In May 2009 he was linked with a future transfer to English club Manchester United to be completed on February 2010. He was spotted during the South American Under-17 championship that Brazil won. After a two-week trial at Carrington.
In December 2010, he joined Bahia on loan for the end of the league campaign.
