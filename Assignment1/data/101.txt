Joaquín 'Ximo' Navarro Jiménez (born 23 January 1990 in Palma, Majorca, Balearic Islands) is a Spanish footballer who plays for Córdoba CF as a right defender.
Navarro graduated from local RCD Mallorca's youth system in 2009. He made his senior debuts with the reserve team in Segunda División B, starting in all games but one over the course of two seasons, and being relegated in his second.
In the 2011 summer, Navarro was loaned to Recreativo de Huelva in Segunda División. He made his official debut for the Andalusians on 27 August, in a 0–1 away loss against Deportivo de La Coruña.
