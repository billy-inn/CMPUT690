Fernando Pacheco Flores (born 18 May 1992 in Badajoz, Extremadura) is a Spanish footballer who plays for Real Madrid C as a goalkeeper.
Pacheco arrived at Real Madrid in 2006, at the age of 14. Five years later, he made his senior debuts, playing with the C team in Tercera División.
Pacheco made his first-team debut on 20 December 2011, replacing Antonio Adán in a 5–1 home win against SD Ponferradina for the season's Copa del Rey (7–1 on aggregate).
