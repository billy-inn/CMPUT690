Marcel Büchel (born 18 March 1991) is an Austrian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Italian side Gubbio, on loan from Siena.
Büchel moved from Swiss side St. Gallen to Italian team Siena in 2009; he signed on loan for Juventus for the 2010-11 season. He made his professional debut for Juventus in the 2010–11 UEFA Europa League, in a match against Austrian team Red Bull Salzburg on 4 November 2010. Büchel made his second first team appearance for Juventus, also in the UEFA Europe League, against Manchester City on 16 December 2010. After the return to Siena, he signed on loan again, this time for Serie B team Gubbio.
