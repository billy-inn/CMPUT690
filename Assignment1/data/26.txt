Francisco 'Paco' Montañés Claverías (born 8 October 1986 in Castellón de la Plana, Valencia) is a Spanish footballer who plays for AD Alcorcón as a midfielder.
Having arrived at FC Barcelona's youth system, Montañés made his debut for the first team on 13 May 2006, playing in the second half of a 2–3 loss at Sevilla FC. He then moved to the lower leagues, with Villarreal CF B and Ontinyent CF.
In August 2010, Montañés joined AD Alcorcón, promoted for the first time to division two.
