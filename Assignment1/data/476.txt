Mario Angel Paglialunga (born 29 October 1988 in Rosario) is an Argentine football defender who currently plays for Calcio Catania in the Italian Serie A.
Paglialunga began his professional career with Argentine club, Rosario Central, making his professional debut in 2006. Paglialunga scored his first goal in Argentine football in a 2-1 defeat by Velez Sarsfield on May 10, 2008. In his first full season with the club, the young midfielder made 19 league appearances scoring two goals. He eventually went on to make 35 additional appearances before attracting the interest of Calcio Catania in the summer of 2011.
Mario officially transferred to the Sicilian outfit July 28, 2011, for an undisclosed transfer fee. He was one of 12 new players arriving at the Stadio Angelo Massimino ahead of the 2011-12 Serie A campaign.
