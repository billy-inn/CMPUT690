Pablo Javier Pérez (born 10 August 1985 in Rosario) is an Argentine football midfielder who currently plays for Newell's Old Boys.
Pérez made his professional debut in a 2-2 draw with Godoy Cruz on 2 December 2006. On 26 May 2007, Pérez scored his first three goals for Newell's with a hat trick in the 4-4 draw with Estudiantes.
In 2009 he left Newell's to join Ecuadorian side Emelec. He was signed by Ecuadorian giants as an attacking option for the midfield and the Ecuadorian pressed even dubbed him the "fantastic" Perez.
