Andrea Palazzi is an Italian footballer who plays for Inter Milan as a midfielder. He has been at Internazionale since he was 10, and is rated by Inter fans as a promising holding midfielder.
He made his professional debut on 6 November 2014, replacing Zdravko Kuzmanović for the last 7 minutes of a 1–1 draw at AS Saint-Étienne in the group stages of the UEFA Europa League.
