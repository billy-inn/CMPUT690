Salvatore Andrea Molina (born January 1, 1992 in Garbagnate Milanese) is an Italian professional football player. Currently, he plays in Lega Pro Prima Divisione U.S. Foggia on loan from Atalanta B.C.
Molina made his debut for Foggia on 14 August 2011, in a Coppa Italia match against Nocerina, acting as a starter.
He represented Italy at the under-20 level in two friendlies: against Switzerland and Poland.
On July 2011, he was loaned to U.S. Foggia, alongside Alessandro De Leidi.
