Joshua Brillante (born 25 March 1993 in Bundaberg) is an Australian football (soccer) player currently playing for Newcastle Jets in the A-League and National Youth League.
Brillante made his A-League debut for Gold Coast on 3 December in a 1-0 loss to the North Queensland Fury.
On 12 May 2012 it was announced he had signed for A-League the Newcastle Jets.
