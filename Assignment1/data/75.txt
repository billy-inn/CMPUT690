Giacomo Bonaventura (born 22 August 1989 in San Severino Marche) is an Italian football (soccer) midfielder. He currently plays for Atalanta.
Bonaventura has made three senior appearances for Atalanta so far in his career including a 4-0 loss in April 2008 to Juventus in a Serie A match.
On January 13, 2009, Atalanta announced he had joined Pergocrema on loan for the remainder of the 2008-09 season.
In June 2010, Padova decided not to buy him outright.
