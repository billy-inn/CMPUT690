Erik Friberg, (born February 10, 1986 in Lindome) is a Swedish footballer who currently plays for Allsvenskan club Malmö FF.
Friberg began his career with Västra Frölunda IF before moving to BK Häcken on January 2007. He made his UEFA Europa League debut on August 2, 2007 against KR Reykjavíkur. The game ended 0–1 for Häcken. He played 20 minutes in two games against Spartak Moscow during the first round of the UEFA Cup.
On December 16, 2010, it was announced that Friberg had signed a deal with Seattle Sounders FC of Major League Soccer. He made his debut for the Sounders in their opening game of the 2011 MLS season against Los Angeles Galaxy.
On 12 December 2011, Seattle Sounders confirmed that Friberg would move to Malmö FF for the 2012 season. Malmö FF announced the day after that Friberg had signed a contract until the end of the 2014 season. He joined Malmö FF when the transfer window opened on 1 January 2012.
