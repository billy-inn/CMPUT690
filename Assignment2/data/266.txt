Lucas Pérez Martínez (born September 10, 1988 in A Coruña) is a Spanish footballer who plays in Ukraine for FC Karpaty Lviv.
A graduate of Atlético Madrid's system, Pérez started his professional career with Rayo Vallecano in Segunda División B and Segunda División in just two seasons.
On January 17, 2011, Pérez as free agent signed a 3-years contract with Ukrainian Premier League's club FC Karpaty Lviv.
He scored his first goal for FC Karpaty Lviv on July 17, 2011, in match against FC Chornomorets Odesa in the first half. The match ended 1-1.
