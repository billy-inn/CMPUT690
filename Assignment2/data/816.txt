Massimo Coda (born 10 November 1988) is an Italian footballer. He plays for Bologna.
Coda started his career at Cavese. At age of 16, he was signed by Bellinzona. He then on loan to Cisco Roma, and Treviso made permanent move for the player. On 26 June 2008, Bologna signed Coda in joint-ownership bid and sent him on loan to Cremonese
