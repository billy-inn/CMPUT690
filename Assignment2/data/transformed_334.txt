PERSON1753 ( Georgian : გიორგი ჭანტურია ) ( born 11 April 1993 ) is a Georgian footballer who plays as a winger for Dutch Eredivisie side ORGANIZATION1668 . PERSON1754 made his competitive debut for ORGANIZATION1668 on the opening day of the 2011–12 Eredivisie season , as a starter in their 0–0 away draw against ORGANIZATION1755 on 7 August . He scored his first goal for the ORGANIZATION1756 side in his second official match , against VVV-Venlo in a 4-0 win .
