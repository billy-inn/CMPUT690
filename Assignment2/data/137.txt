Héctor Yuste Cantón (born 12 January 1988 in Cartagena), simply Héctor, is a Spanish footballer who plays for Cádiz CF on loan from Granada CF, as a midfielder.
Starting off at Fuente Álamo, then with Real Murcia, Héctor finished his football grooming at neighbours FC Cartagena. In 2006, he made his first appearances for the main squad, in Segunda División B.
After one year loaned at lowly AD Las Palas, Héctor returned to Cartagena, helping it return to the second level in 2008–09, finishing the season in the starting XI after a coaching move. In the following campaign, as the Region of Murcia side nearly promoted again, he contributed with 18 matches.
