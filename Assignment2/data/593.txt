Abdoulwahid Sissoko (born 20 March 1990 in Troyes) is a French-Malian footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Brest on loan from Italian club Udinese in Ligue 2. He is the younger brother of Paris St-Germain midfielder Mohamed Sissoko.
Sissoko began his career with Troyes AC and played his debut on 15 August 2008 against Stade Brest in the Ligue 2.
On 1 July 2011, Sissoko signed for Udinese on a five-year deal.
