Roberto Vitiello (born 8 May 1983) is an Italian footballer who plays for Siena in Serie A.
Vitiello started his career at Parma, in 2002, he participated in Torneo di Viareggio with Parma and highly-rated. He then awarded no.34 of the first team.
After loaned to Cesena of Serie C1, he was loaned to Vicenza of Serie B in July 2003. In summer 2004 the deal became permanent. In July 2006 he was sold to Rimini of Serie B.
In June 2010 he was hired by newly relegated Serie B team Siena, signed a 3-year contract.
