Rômulo Souza Orestes Caldeira (Pelotas, 22 May 1987), commonly known as Rômulo, is a Brazilian footballer who plays as right-back. He plays for Serie A club Fiorentina.
On 10 July 2010, Rômulo was presented as a reinforcement of the Cross. The debut for mining took place on 22, against Fluminense.
On 13 April 2011, Rômulo joined Atlético Paranaense on loan from Cruzeiro.
On 29 June 2011, Rômulo joined Fiorentina after his return form Atlético Paranaense to Cruzerio.
