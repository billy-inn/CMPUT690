Mounir Obbadi (born April 4, 1983 in Pontoise, Yvelines) is a French-Moroccan professional footballer, who plays for Troyes AC in the Ligue 2.
Obbadi started his professional career at Paris Saint-Germain, although he never played a first team game. After a years loan spell at Angers SCO he signed there definitively. In January 2007, he joined Ligue 2 club ESTAC, before being loaned back to Angers SCO that season. After ESTAC were relegated, he broke into their first team, and still plays for them. As a playmaker, he is one of the most influent player on the field, and helped the club to climb from National to Ligue 1 over the last three seasons (2010 to 2012).
