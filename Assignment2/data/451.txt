Emerson Palmieri dos Santos (born March 13, 1994 in Santos, (SP), commonly known as Emerson Palmieri) is a Brazilian footballer who plays for Santos FC as a left defender since 2012, when he was promoted from Youth Team.
He made his debut in 21 January 2012 against XV de Piracicaba and he played half time.
In February, he returned to youth team, but in March he won a new chance and was related to the match against Mogi Mirim Esporte Clube
