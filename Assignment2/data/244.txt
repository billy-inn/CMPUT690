Molla Wague (born 21 February 1991 in Verdon) is a French football player who plays for French club Caen in Ligue 1. He plays as a central defender and is a France youth international having represented his nation at under-19 level. Wague made his professional debut on 6 November 2011 in a 3–0 league victory against Dijon. Two weeks later, in his first professional start, he scored his first professional goal in a 2–2 draw with Ajaccio.
