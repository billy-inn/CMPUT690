Ante Vukušić (born 4 June 1991 in Sinj) is a Croatian football striker, currently playing for Hajduk Split in the Prva HNL.
Vukušić started his career playing at youth level for his hometown club Junak Sinj. He joined powerhouse Hajduk Split in 2007, and during his time at the their youth academy was regarded as one of the most promising players.
In January 2009, he signed a professional five-and-a-half year contract with Hajduk keeping him at the club until summer 2014. He made his debut for the first team in a 5–0 win against Croatia Sesvete on 22 April 2009. In the last round of 2008–09 season, he scored his first goal in Prva HNL, a late equalizer against rivals Dinamo Zagreb.
In the 2009–10 season, Ante established himself as the first choice striker at the club. In 22 league appearances, he scored 6 goals and a further 2 goals in 5 cup appearances. His stature at the club at such a young age impressed many of Europe's top clubs and he was set to make a move away from the Croatian club but the management and he mutually chose for him to remain at the club for the following season.
