Rafael Tolói (born 10 October 1990) is a Brazilian football defender who plays for Goiás in the Brazilian Série B. He represents Brazil at under-20 level. He was born in Glória d'Oeste.
He is part of Goiás squad since 2008, and helped his club win the Campeonato Goiano in 2009. As of November 21, 2009, Rafael Tolói played 17 Série B games for Goiás, and scored two goals.
Campeonato Goiano: 2009, 2012
