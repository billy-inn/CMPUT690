Antonio Caracciolo (born June 30, 1990 in Pavia) is an Italian footballer, who plays for Gubbio, on loan from Genoa.
On 29 January 2010 AS Bari signed the defender on loan from Serie C club A.C. Pavia until June 2010. In mid-2010 he was signed by Genoa.
In July 2011 he was loaned to Gubbio but suffered a injury.
