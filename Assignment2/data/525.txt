Michał Chrapek (born 3 April 1992 in Jaworzno) is a Polish footballer who plays as a midfielder for Kolejarz Stróże.
Until 2011 Chrapek played in Młoda Ekstraklasa team of Wisła Kraków. In August 2011, he signed a one-year loan deal with Kolejarz Stróże.
Chrapek played for Poland U-17, U-18 and U-19 national teams.
