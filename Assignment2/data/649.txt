Antonio Vutov (Bulgarian: Антонио Вутов; born 6 June 1996 in Mezdra) is a Bulgarian footballer currently playing as a winger for Levski Sofia.
Born in Mezdra, Vutov began his career at his hometown club, Lokomotiv Mezdra.
In 2011 he joined Levski Sofia Academy. Vutov made his senior debut for Levski on 8 April 2012, in a 1–1 league draw against Lokomotiv Sofia at the age of 15 years and 307 days, making him the youngest player ever to have played for the club.
