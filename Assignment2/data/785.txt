Luís Carlos Ramos Martins (born 10 June 1992) is a Portuguese footballer who plays for S.L. Benfica as a left defender.
Born in Lamego, Viseu District, Martins was promoted to S.L. Benfica's first team for 2011–12. He made his official debut for the club on 2 November 2011, starting in a UEFA Champions League group stage game against FC Basel, a 1–1 draw, and being replaced at the hour-mark by Miguel Vítor.
Martins was picked by Portuguese under-20 coach Ilídio Vale for the 2011 FIFA World Cup in Colombia, helping the national team finish second to Brazil.
