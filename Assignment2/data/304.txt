Francesco Dettori (born 2 March 1983) is an Italian footballer who plays for A.C. ChievoVerona.
He made his Serie B debut on 7 September 2008, whilst playing for Avellino, in a 3-1 defeat away to Triestina.
He joined Chievo on 28 August 2010 in a co-ownership deal for €50,000. On the same day Roberto Inglese also joined Chievo for undisclosed fee and Luca Ariatti moved to Pescara for €650,000.
