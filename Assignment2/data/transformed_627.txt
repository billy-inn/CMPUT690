PERSON3299 ( ( pronounced : [ roˈβeɾto xoˈse roˈsales alˈtuβe ] ) born 20 November 1988 ) is a Venezuelan footballer playing for ORGANIZATION1892 . He normally plays as a defender in a right back position , but can also play on the right side of midfield . PERSON3300 previously played for ORGANIZATION3301 and AA Gent .
