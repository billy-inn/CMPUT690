Luigi Giorgi (born April 19, 1987 in Ascoli Piceno) is an Italian football midfielder or defender, currently on the club of Siena.
Giorgi, started his professional career with Ascoli). In the 2006/2007 season Ascoli loaned him to Foligno on Serie C2 with 26 appareances. In the 2007 season Giorgi return on Serie B with Ascoli.
In 2011, he was sold to Novara for an undisclosed fee.
On January 16, 2012 he is transferred on loan to Siena in Serie A.
